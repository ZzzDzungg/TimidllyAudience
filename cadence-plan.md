## Weekly rhythm

**Monday** — pick the week's source (priority: real Timidlly material from the team; fallback: original educational piece), run the kit, self-edit, send batch for approval.
**Tuesday** — apply feedback, build the carousel in Canva, schedule everything approved.
**Tue–Fri** — pieces go live per the table; each link logged in the calendar the day it publishes.
**Friday** — record last week's 7-day stats into the calendar; 15-minute review: best/worst performer and one hypothesis why; update the Friday-check notes.

## What "running" means (the Week 4 bar)

The cadence counts as running when: two consecutive weeks at ≥N published pieces, every piece logged with its link, and the prior week's stats recorded on time. That's what gets demoed at the Week 5 check — an engine, not a burst.

## Scaling levers (Weeks 5–6, once N is stable)

1. **Second source per week** → N≈10 without new formats
2. **Reshares:** repost the top performer from 2–3 weeks back with a new hook (near-zero cost)
3. **Format expansion** only if data justifies it (e.g., short video) — new formats are expensive; the engine's leverage is repetition, not variety
